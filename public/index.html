Who We Are

Pixel Perfect is a premier provider of home theaters, whole-home audio, security cameras, networking solutions, TVs, and smart home automation. Whether you're looking to create a cinematic experience in your living room, set up a high-performance audio system, or integrate advanced security and networking solutions, we deliver customized, high-quality technology tailored to your needs.

Founded by Chris Prevosto, Zachary Tiso, and Aaron Torelli, our team brings expertise and passion to every project. We serve clients throughout New York and Connecticut, providing exceptional service and cutting-edge technology solutions.
